Unapologetically ME

Built Unapologetically ME from the ground up—designing the entire layout, selecting visuals, and crafting custom typography that embodies unapologetic self-expression. I meticulously created a cohesive design using structured grids and bold imagery, ensuring each page flows seamlessly while making a statement. From photo editing in Photoshop to precise layout work in InDesign, I carefully curated each element to reflect the book's bold, confident message. The result is a coffee table book that not only tells a story but challenges readers to embrace their true selves unapologetically.
