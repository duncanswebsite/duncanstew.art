19 Crimes Wine

Label Design Product Mockup

19 Crimes Wine Special Edition Label

Created a limited edition version of the Snoop Dogg and Martha Stewart wine bottle set for 19 Crimes Wine, designing the label and product mockups. The design blends the brand’s rebellious spirit with a unique twist, incorporating bold typography, custom graphics, and a modern aesthetic. Each label was carefully crafted to capture the personalities of Snoop Dogg and Martha Stewart, while staying true to the 19 Crimes identity. The mockups showcase the bottles in a sleek, eye-catching way, elevating the overall presentation and appeal.
